North American Salon of the Year located in downtown Montreal, Pure hair salon stands out from the crowd thanks to the quality of its haircuts, hair color and specialized services. Anna Pacitto and Daniel Benoît, co-owners of this stunning, seamless space dedicated to beauty, are world renowned hairstylists who are always pushing their craft to new heights collecting the most prestigious hairstyling awards, when they’re not busy offering master class education programs, in Canada and abroad. At Pure, each artist brings a very personal touch to beauty, offering a diversity of styles and interpretations of trends: from the most classic cuts to the most intense hair color palettes, hair at Pure is always done with flawless execution and utmost attention to detail.
